Your privacy and data security are the core foundation of our architecture. We designed our system with a strict Zero-Trust philosophy.

Any sensitive credentials you enter (e.g., SSH passwords, API Keys, Database Root Passwords) are stored ephemerally in local memory variables on your machine. They are never transmitted to our cloud servers, nor are they written to your local disk.
